About me

I'm Fayis, a skilled male web developer and graphics designer hailing from the picturesque state of Kerala. Following my post graduation, I embarked on a transformative journey by enrolling in a prestigious program hosted by the renowned company, Bridgeon. This invaluable experience became the catalyst for refining my knowledge and expertise in the realm of web development.

Throughout my tenure at Bridgeon, I delved deep into the domains of coding and design, immersing myself in the intricacies of the MERN stack. Being both a web developer and graphics designer, I derive immense satisfaction from harmonizing creativity with technical acumen to give life to innovative concepts. The fusion of crafting visually stunning graphics and constructing seamless, user-friendly websites has been the propellant driving my continuous evolution in this field.

With the solid foundation established at Bridgeon and my resolute determination, I stand poised to embrace novel challenges and ventures. My intention is to channel my skills towards crafting impactful digital experiences that etch a lasting imprint on users' minds.
